Privacy Policy

Cosmica Secreta — Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Cosmica Secreta ("we", "our", "the App") is a personal-use application that automates video publishing to TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our application through the TikTok platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the App:

  • TikTok OAuth Token: When you authorize the App via TikTok Login Kit, we receive an access token and refresh token that allow us to publish videos on your behalf.
  • Basic Profile Information: Your TikTok display name and unique user identifier, as provided by the TikTok API during authorization.

We do not collect personal data such as email addresses, phone numbers, browsing history, location data, or any information beyond what TikTok provides through the OAuth flow.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating with the TikTok Content Posting API to upload videos.
  • Maintaining an active session so that video uploads can be performed automatically.

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than the core functionality described above.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored locally in a secure, private database on our server infrastructure.
  • Tokens are never shared with third parties, sold, or transmitted to any external service beyond the TikTok API.
  • Access to stored tokens is restricted to the application owner.
  • We use HTTPS for all communications with the TikTok API.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your information to any third parties. Data is only transmitted to TikTok's API servers as required for video publishing functionality.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as they are needed for the App to function. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8). Upon revocation of access or deletion request, all stored tokens and associated data are permanently removed.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.
  • Request deletion of all data we hold about you by contacting us.
  • Request information about what data we store related to your account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
